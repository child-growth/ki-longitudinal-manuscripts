Methods Detail

Were interested in the causal parameters \(E[Y_a]\) for all values of \(a \in \mathcal{A}\). These parameters represent the mean outcome if, possibly contrary to fact, we intervened to set all units to have \(A=a\). Under the randomization and positivity assumptions, these are identified by the statistical parameters \(\psi_a=E_W[E_{Y|A,W}(Y|A=a,W)]\). In addition, were interested in the mean of \(Y\), \(E[Y]\) under no intervention (the observed mean).
